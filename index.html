Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pocket Blessings ("we", "our", or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Pocket Blessings is a daily Bible verse app that provides personalized verses based on your emotions and spiritual needs.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • User Preferences: Emotion selections, notification preferences
  • Favorites: Bible verses you mark as favorites
  • Usage Statistics: Consecutive days using the app, verses read

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • App Analytics: App crashes, performance data, usage patterns (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Notification Tokens: For delivering push notifications (via OneSignal)

3. How We Store Your Information

All personal data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT store your favorites, preferences, or personal information on external servers. Your data remains private and under your control.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide personalized Bible verses based on your emotional state
  • Save your favorite verses locally on your device
  • Track your daily usage streak
  • Send daily notification reminders (if enabled)
  • Improve app performance and fix crashes
  • Analyze app usage to enhance user experience

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

5.1 Firebase (Google LLC)

  • Purpose: Analytics and crash reporting
  • Data Collected: Device information, app usage, crash logs
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy

5.2 OneSignal

  • Purpose: Push notification delivery
  • Data Collected: Device notification token, notification engagement
  • Privacy Policy: OneSignal Privacy

5.3 RevenueCat

  • Purpose: Purchase analytics and subscription management (for future features)
  • Data Collected: Anonymous user ID, purchase transactions
  • Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

We only share anonymized data with the third-party services mentioned above for the sole purpose of app functionality and improvement.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our app is appropriate for all ages, including children under 13. We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children. All data is stored locally on the device and not transmitted to external servers.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored locally on your device
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to permanently delete all local data
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications in the app settings
  • Data Portability: Your favorites are stored locally and accessible to you at any time

9. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Local storage only (no cloud storage of personal data)
  • Encrypted connections when communicating with third-party services
  • Regular security updates to the app

10. International Data Transfers

Our third-party services (Firebase, OneSignal, RevenueCat) may process data in the United States or other countries. By using the app, you consent to this transfer. These services comply with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do NOT sell data)
  • Request deletion of personal information

12. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Legal basis for processing: Consent and legitimate interest in providing app functionality.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Sending a notification (for major changes)

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
